Description

BuzzBoard powers highly personalized selling based on deep data, machine learning, and expertise on small to mid-sized businesses. By turning data into insights, marketers can identify, segment, and score accounts based on the signals that matters most to their business, including category, competitors, previous touch points, digital footprint, tech stack, and purchase behavior.

This deep and rich pool of data is possible because of our unequalled ability to identify SMB URLs and categories. Our proprietary approach to data intelligence means we can identify at least 3 times as many SMB URLs as other data providers, even from “problem” lists and data sets.

BuzzBoard deploys AI-driven algorithms and tools across all our processes. For example, our Recommendation Engine matches our customers’ products to SMB needs to a much greater extent than other platforms. These capabilities not only help with new sales, but high-value personalization campaigns for up-selling, cross-selling, attrition prevention and win-back marketing.

The bottom line with BuzzBoard : More personalized campaigns that win more business in less time.
